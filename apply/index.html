    Call (301) 638-1800 or visit our office during Monday–Friday, 8:00 AM–5:00 PM (Eastern Time), and a member of our team will help you fill out the application, gather your documents, and answer any questions. There is no need to do this online if you would rather not.

    Other ways to return it

    You do not have to upload anything. You can also return your application — or any documents we ask for — two other ways:

    • In person at our office (12151 Ell Lane, Waldorf, MD 20602), during Monday–Friday, 8:00 AM–5:00 PM (Eastern Time)
    • By mail to the same address

    Documents we may ask for

    To apply, all you need to send is the application itself. After we receive it, our team will contact you and ask for the records that confirm your household and income, such as:

    • Photo identification for each adult in the household
    • Social Security numbers (or documentation) for household members
    • Proof of income — recent pay stubs, a Social Security or benefits award letter, pension or retirement statements
    • Recent bank statements
    • Your Housing Choice Voucher paperwork, if you have one

    When we ask, you can bring the documents in, mail them, or come back to this page and upload them. Missing something? Let us know — we will help you sort it out. Not sure whether you qualify? See if you qualify first.

    What happens next

    • We review your application. We check it over and confirm what we have.
    • We may add you to the waitlist. If a home is not open right now, we place you on the list in the order applications come in. Being on the list does not guarantee a home, and wait times vary.
    • We contact you. Our team will reach out to ask for your verification documents — and again when a home becomes available. We will use the phone number and email you gave us, so please keep them current.

    Questions at any point? Call (301) 638-1800 during Monday–Friday, 8:00 AM–5:00 PM (Eastern Time).

    Submitting an application does not guarantee housing. All applicants must meet the program's eligibility requirements. Homes are offered subject to availability.
