Application (draft) — Village Green Apartments

USDA Rural Development, Section 515 Multifamily Housing. This is a working draft of a modernized, HOTMA-compliant application, shown for review — it replaces the community’s 1996 form. It is not yet accepting online submissions.

DRAFT — not for production use. Content encodes researched HOTMA / 7 CFR 3560.154 requirements (see HANDOFF-USDA-515-APPLICATION.md) and must be reviewed by FMC's RD servicing specialist and a Maryland/Virginia housing attorney before use — especially the screening questions against Maryland/local ban-the-box ordinances, and FMC's rental-history look-back policy.

Before you begin

Please answer every question. If something does not apply to you, write “N/A.” Every adult (18 or older) who will live in the home must sign.

You can also apply on paper. If you need help, or need this application in another format, please call us; we are glad to help.

If there is a waitlist, your priority is set by the date and time we receive a COMPLETE application. If anything is missing, we will contact you within 10 days to let you know.

Your rights under the Violence Against Women Act (VAWA)

Federal law protects applicants and tenants who are victims of domestic violence, dating violence, sexual assault, or stalking. You cannot be denied housing, or have your tenancy ended, because you are or have been a victim of these crimes. You have the right to confidentiality and to certain protections and emergency transfers.

You will receive the full VAWA notice (HUD Form 5380) and self-certification form (HUD Form 5382) with your tenancy paperwork, and you may ask us for them at any time.
